Moderator Questions
Brett, the era of the 'slop bowl' seems to be drawing to a close. Why is CAVA holding onto the bowl?
Your stock has done well, in part because you've kept a tight grip on margins. How?
You eliminated tipping in favor of higher wages. Most operators say this model doesn't work at scale. Which is it?
CAVA's growth model is built on adding restaurants. This feels like the worst time to do that. Are you slowing the pace?
You've told the Street you'll take only 'very modest' price increases. How much more do you expect CAVA's menu prices to go up before you start losing customers?
You are supposed to be affordable. How can you seriously say an $18 bowl is affordable?
